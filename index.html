About the maker

Handcrafted pieces with a personal touch.

Karen's Custom Creations is built around the joy of making jewelry that feels meaningful, beautiful, and one-of-a-kind. Each piece is created by hand with careful attention to color, texture, shape, and detail.

Inspired by nature, the shoreline, and the changing seasons, Karen creates pieces that bring beauty into everyday life.

Shipping & returns

Packaged with care.

Most orders ship within 3–5 business days unless otherwise noted. Every piece is packaged carefully so it arrives ready to wear or gift.

Because many items are handmade and one-of-a-kind, returns are handled individually. Please contact Karen within 7 days of delivery if there is an issue with your order.
